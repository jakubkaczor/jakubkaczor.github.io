on my personal website! This project is experimental. I’m not sure about its destiny. For now I will be using it to share public information about me, such as contact information, platforms you can find me on, etc. It is possible I will be experimenting with blogging, so you can subscribe to my feed here.

Design

My loved one has told me that this website looked “raw”. Just like I had been too lazy to do it. Firstly, I was surprised. To my eyes it has never looked like this. Only later I understood that it may seem like this indeed. Perhaps at first I’d thought differently, because one of my the most visited sites is Wikipedia.

This website – as Wikipedia, I believe – is designed to be informational with simplicity in mind. I wanted it to be

  • easy to maintain,
  • content focused,
  • informational,
  • responsive,
  • loading fast.
