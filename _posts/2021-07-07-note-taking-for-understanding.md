# Expressing thoughts
Have you experienced this situation, when you think you've got a great
idea, solution to the problem, but when it came to referring it, it turned
out completely off? I bet you have, so have I! It is kind of weird, isn't
it? We have an idea, moreover, we have a clear image of it right in our
minds, but we only realize its shortcomings only after expressing it in
words.

# Note-taking
I have never been note-taker and never paid much attention to it. In the
first year of my studies of mathematics, when the problems I dealt with
started to more hardly demand coherent thinking, when the words became the
majority of the work instead of numbers, I realized the possible value of
it, because of the phenomenon described in the previous paragraph.

It seems like the process of creating notes is largely more beneficial,
than just having them. Despite this it appears that having someone else's
view on the matter can be very helpful to understand it. Therefore, I think
when you struggle to understand some concept it may be a good idea to make
a note of it and share with others. This is one of the prospects I have for
this blog.
